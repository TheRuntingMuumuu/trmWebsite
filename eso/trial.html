What are trials

ESO has a lot of content. Some of that content, is trials. Trials are big hard 12 player dungeons. They cannot be done with any less then 12 players. They have 2 modes. Normal and veteran (and veteran hard mode). Normal can be done by anyone who is decent, but veteran ones are much harder (I have not done any.)
