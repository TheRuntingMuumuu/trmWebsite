ADDR4 ROUTERS
My name is Owen and I am the leader of the ADDR4 Routers clan (Clash Royale, Video Game). This website is here to share the rules and some information about the clan since it is a bit hard to do in Clash Royale. This page will have the rules, and some other information, but more detailed (and better looking) ones can be found on the discord.
Rules
The Rules are simple
  • Be Nice
  • Be Respectful
  • Be Appropriate>
  • Be Active (play AT LEAST once every week)
  • Participate in the war (see below)
  • Keep the language appropriate
  • HAVE FUN
War
For the war, I want everyone to try to participate every week, but I understand that sometimes things happen and you cannot participate. So, the rule for the clan is that everyone must participate at least ONCE every 2 weeks. It can take as little as 30 seconds to participate if you start a clan battle, and let the enemy win. Also, as incentive for people to participate, if you participate for 5 weeks in a row, you will get the ELDER rank.
